# CEO Compensation 2024

Beginning in 2017, public companies have been required to disclose the ratio of CEO pay to median worker pay. This is the 2024 data from the AFL/CIO for companies in the S&P 500.

The data are in `CEO_Compensation_2024.txt`

The variables in this dataset are: 

Header | Description
---|--------
`Ticker` | Stock symbol
`Company` | Company name 
`Med.Worker.Pay` | Median worker pay in dollars (time frame not reported, but seems to be annual) 
`Pay.Ratio` | Ratio of CEO pay to median worker pay 
`CEO.Pay.M.` | CEO pay (millions of dollars) 
`mid.68` | Indicator for whether this CEO pay value is within +/- 1 SD of the mean CEO pay 

This dataset is sourced from: https://www.niaaa.nih.gov/sites/default/files/pcyr1970-2022.txt
