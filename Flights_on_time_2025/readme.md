# Flights on time 2025

The Bureau of Transportation Statistics of the U.S. Department of Transportation publishes information about airline performance. The data report the percentage of flights departing on time each month from January 1994 through September 2025. Note the effects of the pandemic.

The data are in `Flights_on_time_2025.txt`

The variables in this dataset are: 

Header | Description
---|--------
`Year` | Decimal year indicating month and year of interest  
`Month` | Month 
`Ontime.Arrivals` | Number of flights that arrived on time 
`Ontime.pct` | Percent of flights that arrived on time 
`Arrival.Delays` | Number of flights that arrived late 
`Delayed.pct` | Percent of flights that arrived late 
`Flights.Cancelled` | Number of flights cancelled
`Cancelled.Pct` | Percent of flights cancelled 
`Diverted` | Number of flights diverted 
`Flight.Operations` | Total number of flights (scheduled to) arrive 
`Groups` | x indicates flights from Oct 2019-July 2020 (a range which includes the height of the COVID pandemic) 

This dataset is sourced from: http://www.transtats.bts.gov/HomeDrillChart.asp?URL_SelectYear=2019&URL_SelectMonth=6&URL_Time=1&URL_Selection=1  https://www.transtats.bts.gov/HomeDrillChart_Month.asp
