# Flights on time 2025

The Bureau of Transportation Statistics of the U.S. Department of Transportation publishes information about airline performance. The data report the percentage of flights departing on time each month from January 1994 through September 2020. Note the effects of the pandemic.

The data are in `Flights_on_time_2025.txt`

The variables in this dataset are: 

Header | Description
---|--------
`Year` | 
`Month` | 
`Onetime.Arrivals` | 
`Ontime.pct` | 
`Arrival.Delays` | 
`Delayed.pct` | 
`Flights.Cancelled` | 
`Cancelled.Pct` | 
`Diverted` | 
`Flight.Operations` | 
`Groups` | 

This dataset is sourced from: https://www.niaaa.nih.gov/sites/default/files/pcyr1970-2022.txt
