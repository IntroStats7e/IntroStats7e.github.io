# Receivers 2024

NFL data from the 2024 football season reported the number of yards gained by all of the league’s receivers.

The data are in `Receivers_2024.txt`

The variables in this dataset are:

---|--------

`Team` | 
`Position` | 
`GamesPlayed` | 
`Receptions` | 
`RecTargets` | 
`Yards` | 
`AvgYds` | 
`TDs` | 
`LongRec` | 
`Plays20Plus` | 
`YdsPerGame` | 
`Fumbles` | 
`FumblesLost` | 
`YdsAfterCatch` | 
`FirstDowns` | 

This dataset is sourced from: https://www.espn.com/nfl/stats/player/_/stat/receiving/season/2024/seasontype/2
