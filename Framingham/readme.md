# Framingham

The Framingham Heart Study is one of the longest running health studies. It has followed original subjects, their children, and their grand children, looking for factors that affect cardiac health. These data only include subjects whose cholesterol was measured in the first exam. Source:  "Statistical Methods in Epidemiology" by H.A.Kahn and C.T.Sempos

The data are in `Framingham.txt`

The variables in this dataset are: 

Header | Description
---|--------
`Cholesterol` | Serum cholesterol at first exam 
`Age` | Age at first exam 
`Sex` | Binary sex (MALE or FEMALE) 
`SBP` | Systolic blood pressure at first exam 
`DBP` | Diastolic blood pressure at first exam 
`CIG` | Number of cigarettes smoked per day at first exam 

This dataset is sourced from:  "Statistical Methods in Epidemiology" by H.A.Kahn and C.T.Sempos
