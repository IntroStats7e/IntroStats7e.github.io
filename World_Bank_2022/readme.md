# World Bank 2022

The World Bank tracks global development data. This dataset includes a variety of indices from 2022 (except education variables, which are from 2015). 

The data are in `World_Bank_2022.txt`

The variables in this dataset are: 

Header | Description
---|--------
`country` | 
`MenYrsEduc_2015` | 
`WomenYrsEduc_2015` | 
`YrsEducWomenPctMen_2015` | 
`CellsPer100_2022` | 
`ChildMortalityPer1000_2022` | 
`Gini_2022` | 
`InternetPct_2022` | 
`LFParticipation_2019` | 
`LifeExp_2022` | 
`HHPerCapIncome_2022` | 
`VaccineRate_2022` | 
`WaterAccessPct_2022` |
