# Saratoga Houses 2025

Prices of homes in Saratoga, NY in 2003 and 2025, along with facts about them. Good basis for multiple regressions to predict the Price of the house. Several predictors are collinear.

The data are in `Saratoga_Houses_2025.txt`


This dataset is sourced from: https://www.niaaa.nih.gov/sites/default/files/pcyr1970-2022.txt
