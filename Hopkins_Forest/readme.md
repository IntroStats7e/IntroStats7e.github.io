# Hopkins Forest

The Hopkins Memorial Forest is a 2500-acre reserve in Massachusetts, New York, and Vermont managed by the Williams College Center for Environmental Studies (CES). As part of its mission, the CES monitors forest resources and conditions over the long term. This dataset includes climatological variables for each day of the year 2011. 

The data are in `Hopkins_Forest.txt`

The variables in this dataset are: 

Header | Description
---|--------
`Date` | Date in MM/DD/YYYY format 
`Year` | Year (2011 for every entry in this dataset) 
`Month` | Numeric month
`Day` | Numeric day of month
`Day.of.Year` | Numeric day of year 
`Avg.Temp.C` | Average temperature recorded (degrees C) 
`Max.Temp.C` | Maximum temperature recorded (degrees C) 
`Min.Temp.C` | Minimum temperature recorded (degrees C) 
`Avg.Temp.F` | Average temperature recorded (degrees F) 
`Max.Temp.F` | Maximum temperature recorded (degrees F) 
`Min.Temp.F` | Minimum temperature recorded (degrees F) 
`Avg.Rel.Hum.Pct` | Average relative humidity (%) 
`Max.Rel.Hum.Pct` | Maximum relative humidity (%) 
`Min.Rel.Hum.Pct` | Minimum relative humidity (%) 
`Avg.Sol.Rad` | Average solar radiation (watts per square meter, w/m^2)
`Max.Sol.Rad` | Maximum solar radiation (w/m^2)
`Min.Sol.Rad` | Minimum solar radiation (w/m^2) 
`Total.Sol.Rad` | Total solar radiation (w/m^2) 
`Avg.Wind.mph` | Average wind speed (miles per hour, mph) 
`Max.Wind.mph` | Maximum wind speed (mph) 
`Min.Wind.mph` | Minimum wind speed (mph) 
`Avg.Barom.mb` | Average barometric pressure (millibars, mb) 
`Max.Barom.mb` | Maximum barometric pressure (mb)  
`Min.Barom.mb` | Minimum barometric pressure (mb) 
`Precip.in` | Inches of precipitation 
`Deep.Well.ft` | Deep well water level (ft relative to surface) 
`Shallow.Well.ft` | Shallow well water level (ft relative to surface)
`Soil.Temp.80.cm.C` | Soil temperature 80 cm below surface (degrees C)
`Soil.Temp.10.cm.C` | Soil temperature 10 cm below surface (degrees C) 

This dataset is sourced from: hmf.williams.edu/researchacademics/data/
