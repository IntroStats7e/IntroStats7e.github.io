# OKCupid CatsDogs

OkCupid, one of the largest online dating sites in the world, has about a million daily active users, 75% of whom are seeking a relationship. This dataset includes only pet-owning OkCupid users and includes information about their gender, their pet ownership (cats, dogs, or both), and whether or not their profile indicates that they use drugs or smoke. 

The data are in `OKCupid_CatsDogs.txt`

The variables in this dataset are: 

Header | Description
---|--------
`CatsDogsBoth` | Pet ownership 
`Gender` | Binary gender (man or woman) 
`drugsY.N` | Whether or not the profile indicates drug use 
`smokesY.N` | Whether or not the profile indicates smoking 

This dataset is sourced from: OKCupid.com
