# Hurricanes 2024

The barometric pressure at the center of a hurricane is often used to measure the strength of the hurricane because it can predict the maximum wind speed of the storm. How well is the wind speed predicted by the barometric pressure? The data record all hurricanes on record 1851 - 2023.

The data are in `Hurricanes_2024.txt`

The variables in this dataset are: 

Header | Description
---|--------
`Year` | 
`month` | 
`Saffir.Simpson` | 
`Central_Pressure` | 
`Max_wind` | 
`name` |
