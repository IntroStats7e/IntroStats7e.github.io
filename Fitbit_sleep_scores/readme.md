# Fitbit sleep scores

One of the contributing authors recorded their sleep quality data over the course of 113 nights from their Fitbit activity tracking wristband. 

The data are in `Fitbit_sleep_scores.txt`

The variables in this dataset are: 

Header | Description
---|--------
`timestamp` | Date/time that sleep score was recorded
`overall_score` | Total sleep score (out of 100); sum of composition, revitalization, and duration scores
`composition_score` | Score out of 25; higher indicates more time spent in deep and REM sleep 
`revitalization_score` | Score out of 25 summarizing how restful your sleep was (based on sleeping heart rate, resting heart rate, and restlessness)
`duration_score` | Score out of 50; compares your time asleep vs. awake to your goal 
`deep_sleep_in_minutes` | Minutes of deep sleep 
`resting_heart_rate` | Resting heart rate (beats per minute) 
`restlessness` | Percentage of the night spent tossing and turning
