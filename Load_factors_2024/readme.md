# Load factors 2024

The Research and Innovative Technology Administration of the Bureau of Transportation Statistics reports load factors (passenger-miles as a percentage of available seat miles) for commercial airlines for every month from October 2002 through September 2024 for both domestic and international flights.

The data are in `Load_factors_2024.txt`

The variables in this dataset are:
