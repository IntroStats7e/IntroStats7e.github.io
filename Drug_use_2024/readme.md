# Drug use 2024

The European Union Drugs Agency regularly surveys students aged 15- to 16-years old across many European countries in a project called the European School Survey Project on Alcohol and Other Drugs (ESPAD). The 2024 survey was conducted among 113,882 students across 37 European countries. 

The data are in `Drug_use_2024.txt`

The variables in this dataset are:

This dataset is sourced from: European Union Drugs Agency - 2024 European School Survey Project on Alcohol and Other Drugs (ESPAD), https://www.euda.europa.eu/publications/data-factsheets/espad-2024-key-findings_en#source-data.
