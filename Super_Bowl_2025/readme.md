# Super Bowl 2025

For each Super Bowl game, this dataset includes the name of the winning team, their score, the name of the losing team, their score, the total number of points scored during the game, and the "edge" (the amount by which the winning team won the game). 

The data are in `Super_Bowl_2025.txt`

The variables in this dataset are: 

Header | Description
---|--------
`Super.Bowl` | Identifies Super Bowl year by Roman numerals
`Winner` | Winning team's city 
`Winners.Score` | Winning team's score
`Loser` | Losing team's city
`Losers.Score` | Losing team's name 
`Points` | Total number of points scored by both teams 
`Edge` | Difference between winning and losing teams' scores

This dataset is sourced from: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_Super_Bowl_champions
