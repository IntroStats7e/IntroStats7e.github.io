# Gas prices 2024

Monthly gas prices for all grades and all formulations ($/gallon) in the United States as reported by the U.S. Energy Information Administration for 1993 through December 2024. Prices are available at the cite for all weeks. Data here are for the final week of each month.

The data are in `Gas_prices_2024.txt`

The variables in this dataset are: 

Header | Description
---|--------
`Year` | Year 
`Month` | Name of month
`Price` | Gas price in final week of month (dollars) 
`Date` | Date in MM/DD/YYYY format 
`Num.month` | Month in numeric format 

This dataset is sourced from: https://www.eia.gov/dnav/pet/hist/LeafHandler.ashx?n=PET&s=EMM_EPM0_PTE_NUS_DPG&f=W
