# Gas prices 2024

Monthly gas prices for all grades and all formulations ($/gallon) in the United States as reported by the U.S. Energy Information Administration for 1993 through October 2019. Prices are available at the cite for all weeks. Data here are for the final week of each month.

The data are in `Gas_prices_2024.txt`

The variables in this dataset are:

`Month` | 
`Price` |
