# Heart attack stays

Number of days spent in hospital by female patients admitted to hospitals in New York during one year with a primary diagnosis of acute myocardial infarction (heart attack). Data are from public medicare records. Consider the distribution of stays. The data also include the age of the patient

The data are in `Heart_attack_stays.txt`

The variables in this dataset are:

`Stay.days.` | 
`Age.years.` |
