# Election 2020 2024

The results of U.S. presidential elections are dissected for years afterwards (by both major political parties). This dataset includes county-level voter and demographic information to explore voter turnout and election results by county or state. 

The data are in `Election_2020_2024.txt`


This dataset is sourced from: https://www.niaaa.nih.gov/sites/default/files/pcyr1970-2022.txt
