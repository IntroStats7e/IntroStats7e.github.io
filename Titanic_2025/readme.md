# Titanic 2025

A complete and corrected dataset listing all those aboard HMS Titanic and including related facts about them including Age, how much they paid for their ticket, which boat they survived in (if they survived), and their job if they were crew members. Stories, biographies and pictures can be found on the site: www.encyclopedia-titanica.org/. This version is corrected since the version posted previously as errors are discovered and additional information is gathered. The data are correct as of 2025.

The data are in `Titanic_2025.txt`
