# Slalom times 2022

The Men’s Giant Slalom skiing event consists of two runs whose times are added together for a final score. The data give the giant slalom times in the 2022 Winter Olympics at Yanqing.

The data are in `Slalom_times_2022.txt`

The variables in this dataset are: 

Header | Description
---|--------
`Rank` | 
`Bib` | 
`Name` | 
`Country` | 
`Run.1..sec.` | 
`Run.2..sec.` | 
`Total..sec.` | 
`Total` | 
`Behind` |
