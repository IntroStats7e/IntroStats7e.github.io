# Occupational Prestige 2025

What determines how prestigious a job is? This file includes prestige ratings for 1047 occupations in 1989 and 2012 (based on ratings in the General Social Survey, or GSS), and in 2016 (using a new system developed in 2024 and presented in the manuscript https://doi.org/10.1525/collabra.92882), along with mean and median salary and number of people working in that job as of May 2023 from the Bureau of Labor Statistics. 

The data are in `Occupational_Prestige_2025.txt`

The variables in this dataset are:
