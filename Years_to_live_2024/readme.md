# Years to live 2024

Insurance companies and other organizations use actuarial tables to estimate the remaining lifespans of their customers. The data file gives estimated life expectancy (additional years of life) for people in the United States, according to the actuarial tables of the SSA.

The data are in `Years_to_live_2024.txt`

The variables in this dataset are:
