# Cost of living countries 2025

Cost of living for countries around the world, as measured by price of common goods (eggs, bottled water, white bread). 

The data are in `Cost_of_living_countries_2025.txt`

The variables in this dataset are: 

Header | Description
---|--------
`Country` | Country name
`Loaf.of.Fresh.White.Bread..500g.` | Average cost of a loaf of white bread
`Eggs..regular...12.` | Average cost of a dozen eggs
`Water..1.5.liter.bottle.` | Average cost of a 1.5 l bottle of water

This dataset is sourced from: https://www.niaaa.nih.gov/sites/default/files/pcyr1970-2022.txt
