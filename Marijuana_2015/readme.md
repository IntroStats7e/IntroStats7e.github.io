# Marijuana 2015

In 2015 the Council of Europe published a report entitled The European School Survey Project on Alcohol and Other Drugs (www.espad.org). Among other issues, the survey investigated the percent-ages of 16-year-olds who had used marijuana. The data are the results for 38 European countries.

The data are in `Marijuana_2015.txt`
