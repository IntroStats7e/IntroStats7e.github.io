# Doctors and life expectancy

Life expectancy at birth, TV's per capita, and doctor's per capita for countries of the world. Doctors predict life expectancy, but is that causal? TVs also predict life expectancy.

The data are in `Doctors_and_life_expectancy.txt`


This dataset is sourced from: https://www.niaaa.nih.gov/sites/default/files/pcyr1970-2022.txt
