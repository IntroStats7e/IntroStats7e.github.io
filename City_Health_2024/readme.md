# City Health 2024

The Department of Population Health at NYU Langone Health and the Robert F. Wagner School of Public Service at NYU created the City Health Dashboard as a health improvement planning resource for U.S. cities. The dataset can be used to investigate associations between various measures of physical, environmental, social, and economic well-being across U.S. cities.  

The data are in `City_Health_2024.txt`
