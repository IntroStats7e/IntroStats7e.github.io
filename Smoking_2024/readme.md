# Smoking 2024

The Gallup poll tracks cigarette smoking in the United States. How has the percentage of people who smoke changed since the danger became clear during the last half of the 20th century? The data give percentages of smokers  as estimated by surveys, from 1944 through 2018.

The data are in `Smoking_2024.txt`
