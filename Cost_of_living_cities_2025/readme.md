# Cost of living cities 2025

The Cost of Living Index (COL) summarizes the cost of a representative market basket of goods that includes groceries, COL to determine living allowances and salaries for employees. Inflation is often measured by how much the COL changes from year to year. Relative COLs can be found for different cities. We have data giving COL components relative to New York City. For New York City, each index is 100(%).

The data are in `Cost_of_living_cities_2025.txt`

The variables in this dataset are: 

Header | Description
---|--------
`City` | City name
`Eggs..regular...12.` | Cost of a dozen eggs 
`Water..1.5.l.` | Cost of 1.5 l bottle of water
`Bread..white.` | Cost of a loaf of white bread

This dataset is sourced from: https://www.niaaa.nih.gov/sites/default/files/pcyr1970-2022.txt
