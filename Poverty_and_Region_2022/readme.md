# Poverty and Region 2022

Data were reported by the U.S. Census Bureau. The data show the number of people (in thousands) living above and below the poverty line in each of the four regions of the United States.

The data are in `Poverty_and_Region_2022.txt`

The variables in this dataset are:

---|--------
`Region` | 
`Below` | 
`Above` | 
`Total.Thousands.` |
