# Baseball 2024

Summaries of the performance of each of the major league baseball teams in 2024. 

The data are in `Baseball_2024.txt`

The variables in this dataset are: 

Header | Description
---|--------
`Team` | Team name 
`TM` | Team abbreviation 
`League` | Team's league 
`Won` | Number of games won in 2024
`Lost` | Number of games lost in 2024
`BatAge` | Batters' average age (years)
`Runs.per.game` | Average runs per game 
`HR.per.game` | Average home runs per game 
`AB` | At-bats 
`Runs` | Runs 
`Hits` | Hits
`X2B` | Doubles 
`X3B` | Triples
`HR` | Home runs 
`RBI` | Runs batted in
`StolenB` | Stolen bases
`CaughtS` | Time caught stealing
`BB` | Bases on Balls 
`SO` | Strike-outs 
`BAvg` | Batting average (hits per at-bats) 
`OBP` | On-base percentage
`SLG` | Slugging percentage
`OPS` | On base plus slugging 
`TB` | Total bases
`GDP` | Double plays grounded into 
`HBP` | Times hit by pitch 
`SH` | Sacrifice hits
`SF` | Sacrifice flies
`IBB` | Intentional base on balls 
`LOB` | Runners left on base 
`Rank` | Team rank 
`Home.Games` | Number of home games played 
`Home.Total.Att` | Total attendance at all home games 
`Home.Avg.Att` | Average attendance at home games 
`Road.Games` | Number of road games played
`Road.Avg.Att` | Average attendance at road games 
`Overall.Games` | Overall games played
`Overall.Avg.Att` | Overall average attendance

This dataset is sourced from: https://www.niaaa.nih.gov/sites/default/files/pcyr1970-2022.txt
