# Baseball 2024

Summaries of the performance of each of the major league baseball teams in 2024. Variables include: Games won, Games Lost, Runs peer game, At bats, Runs, Hits, Doubles, Triples, Home runs, Runs batted in, Bases stolen, Time caught stealing, Bases on Balls, Strikeouts, Hits/At Bats, On Base Percentage, Slugging percentage, On base+Slugging, Total bases, Double plays grounded into, Times hit by pitch, Sacrifice hits, Sacrifice flies, Intentional base on balls, and Runners Left On Base

The data are in `Baseball_2024.txt`
