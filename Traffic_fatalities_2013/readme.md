# Traffic fatalities 2013

Traffic fatalities in a variety of vehicles and for a variety of situations for the years from 1975 to 2013. These are multiple time series, but can also be related to each other.

The data are in `Traffic_fatalities_2013.txt`

The variables in this dataset are:

---|---------

`Occupant.fatalities` | 
`Passenger.car.fatalities` | 
`Subcompact` | 
`Compact` |

`Other.Unknown` | 
`Truck.total` | 
`Light` | 
`Large` |

`Motorcycle` |
